About Us

Welcome to Kaliyamman Hays Logistics & Transport!

Established in 2015, Kaliyamman Hays Logistics & Transport began as a product-selling company dedicated to delivering high-quality goods and services. Over the years, we have grown and transformed into a leading small-scale industry, now specializing in comprehensive transportation and logistics solutions.

Founded by M. Murugan, our company is built on a foundation of excellence and a steadfast commitment to customer satisfaction. Under Mr. Murugan's visionary leadership, we have broadened our scope to offer a diverse range of logistics services tailored to meet the varied needs of our clients.

Our services include:

  • Efficient Freight Transportation: Ensuring timely and secure delivery of goods across various locations.
  • Logistics Management: Streamlining processes to optimize supply chain efficiency.
  • Express Shipping: Offering rapid delivery options to meet urgent requirements.

At Kaliyamman Hays Logistics & Transport, we blend extensive industry experience with cutting-edge solutions to deliver dependable, timely, and cost-effective logistics services. Our mission is to support businesses and retailers by offering top-tier transportation solutions while maintaining the highest levels of professionalism and integrity.

We are dedicated to enhancing your logistics experience and look forward to serving you with unparalleled expertise and commitment.
