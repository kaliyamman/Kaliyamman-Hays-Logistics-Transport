About Us

Kaliyamman Hays Logistics & Transport was established in 2018, initially as a product-selling company focused on delivering quality goods and services to our valued customers. Over the years, we have evolved into a dynamic small-scale industry specializing in comprehensive transportation and logistics solutions. Founded by M. Murugan, our company prides itself on its commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction. Under M. Murugan’s leadership, we have expanded our offerings to include a wide range of logistics services designed to meet the diverse needs of our clients. From efficient freight transportation and logistics management to secure warehousing and express shipping, we ensure that every aspect of our service is executed with precision and care. At Kaliyamman Hays Logistics & Transport, we combine our deep industry expertise with innovative solutions to provide reliable, timely, and cost-effective logistics services. Our mission is to support businesses and retailers by delivering top-notch transportation solutions while upholding the highest standards of professionalism and integrity.
